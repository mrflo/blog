An inconvenient truth

October 10 2006, 1:20 AM  by Xavier Damman
The movie is just right now in theater in Belgium and you should watch it. I saw it in the plane from Hong Kong to London one month ago and it's very interesting. For sure we can criticize the movie for many points such as - This is very an american movie: they emphasize the principal character (Al Gore) a bit too much. They like glorifying people, for their proudness. - Every 5 minutes you get an ad for Apple Computers :-D but these flaws just fade away compares to the importance of the topic. Al Gore is definitely a very good orator and at the end he's doing a very honorable job with that movie.
