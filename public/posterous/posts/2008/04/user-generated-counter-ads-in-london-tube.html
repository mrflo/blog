User generated counter ads in London tube

April 12 2008, 8:33 AM  by Xavier Damman

User generated counter ads in London tube
Mise en ligne par xdamman
This picture has been taken in the London tube.
A probably not so happy client of Tiscali tagged other advertises with
a hand made sticker "don't get Tiscali broadband. They get your money
but don't connect you".

I love it.
This is all in a web2.0 fashion where power is shifted to individuals.

Nowadays you should better pay attention to your reputation because
anyone could make a dent to it!

And I think it is a great trend because before companies would only
respect very high profile clients or media representatives. .
And I know what I'm talking about because I used to use the privilege
of owning a media (distributed in 30k copies in Belgium). Strangely
enough, most of client services were taking a special care of us.
Now they will have to take care of everybody. That's much more fair.
